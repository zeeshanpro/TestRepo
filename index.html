About Us

TITLIS Pharma is based in Lahore ; the city known as heart of Pakistan . The company is formed by team of experience professionals from pharmaceutical industry led by Syed Faisal Zaman ; whose mission and vision have resulted in establishment of state of the art manufacturing facility at Sundar Industrial Estate, Raiwind Road, Lahore.

We are engaged in manufacturing of high quality finished dosage forms, marketing and import /export of all type of pharmaceuticals. We are also doing Contract Manufacturing for some leading pharmaceutical companies.

Our social and environmental policies ensure that the quality and the safety of our products are always coupled with the health and safety of our people.

Our Mission & Vision

We shall discover, develop and successfully market pharmaceutical products to prevent, diagnose, alleviate and cure diseases.

We shall provide total customer satisfaction and achieve leadership in chosen markets, products and services across the globe, through excellence in technology, based on world-class research and development.

We are responsible to the society. We shall be good corporate citizens and will be driven by high ethical standards in our practices.
